# Question:
"I signed up with the "Free Trial" account. How much resources do I get? When do they expire? How do I check remaining?"

A.I.Answer:
>"Free trial resources are provided as **trial points**, not fixed resource allocations. Here’s what you need to know:

- Trial points are valid for **one year** from the top of the hour when you claim them (e.g., claimed at 10:05 → effective at 10:00 that day, expire exactly one year later).
- Each solution trial can run for **up to 168 hours total**; you cannot exceed this duration even with sufficient points.
- Trial resources are created under a **dedicated trial account** and are **deleted permanently** when the trial ends, expires, or points run out—data cannot be recovered.
- To check your remaining trial points and expiration: go to **Expenses and Costs console > My Trial > Solution Free Trial**.

Note: Trial points may be partially deducted even if resource creation fails, because multi-product solutions charge only for successfully created components.
